COMPANY OVERVIEW
----------------
SkillAgents AI is an AI-powered workforce training platform built for enterprises. We deploy intelligent AI agents that learn your company's tools, workflows, and knowledge base — then train your employees through personalized, role-specific learning paths. Unlike traditional LMS platforms that serve static video libraries, SkillAgents AI creates a living training system that adapts as your organization evolves.

Founded: 2023
HQ: San Francisco, CA
Stage: Series B ($28M raised)
Customers: 40+ enterprise customers across financial services, healthcare, and tech

CORE VALUE PROPOSITIONS
------------------------
1. 60% Faster AI Skill Adoption — employees reach proficiency on new AI tools in weeks, not quarters
2. Role-Specific Learning Paths — AI agents customize content for each job function (engineer, analyst, manager, ops)
3. Skills Gap Analysis Dashboard — real-time visibility into which teams are AI-ready and which are falling behind
4. Measurable L&D ROI — track productivity lift, tool adoption rates, and certification completion with hard numbers
5. Compliance Training Automation — automate mandatory compliance and policy training, with audit trails
6. Zero Integration Headache — works alongside existing tools; no ripping out current systems

HOW IT WORKS
-------------
Step 1: Onboarding — We ingest your internal docs, SOPs, tools, and workflows into our AI knowledge graph
Step 2: Assessment — AI identifies skills gaps per role and creates a baseline for each employee
Step 3: Training — AI agents deliver bite-sized, contextual training directly in Slack, Teams, or browser
Step 4: Measurement — Dashboard tracks adoption, completion, and productivity lift weekly
